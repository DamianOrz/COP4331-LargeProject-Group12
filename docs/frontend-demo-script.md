# Frontend Demo Script

1. Open `/register` and submit an empty form to show visible validation errors.
2. Enter an invalid email and weak password on `/register` to show email and password rules.
3. Open `/login` and submit an empty form to show required email and password errors.
4. Open `/forgot-password`, submit an empty email, then enter an email to show reset feedback.
5. Open `/dashboard` and point out recipe count, planned meal count, quick actions, and the weekly summary.
6. Open `/recipes` and show mock recipe cards.
7. Search for `Turkey` to show recipe filtering.
8. Open `/recipes/new`, create a recipe, and show it in the recipe list.
9. Edit the created recipe from `/recipes/:id/edit` and show the updated list entry.
10. Delete the created recipe from `/recipes`.
11. Open `/planner` and show Monday through Sunday grouping.
12. Assign a recipe to a weekday and show the planner update.
13. Open `/random-route` to show the 404 page.
14. Resize to a mobile-width viewport and show that the layout remains usable without horizontal overflow.
